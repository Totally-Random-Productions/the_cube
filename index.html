FAQ

What is DRGN Studios?

DRGN studios is small indie company located in Barbados with the goal of creating video games for everyone to enjoy.

Why do we make Games?

We make games to not only challenge individuals but for them to have fun online without the need to download any application especially during the state of the world.

What was our first game developed in?

Our first game, The CUBE, was developed in phaser. Phaser is a 2D game framework used for making HTML5 games for desktop and mobile.

Are we currently hiring?

Yes, DRGN studios is looking to hire sound designers and play testers.
